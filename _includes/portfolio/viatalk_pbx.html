ViaTalk PBX

PHP, CodeIgniter, Javascript, jQuery, MySQL, CSS

I developed ViaTalk PBX, a VoIP and web application used by ViaTalk, HostRocket, and several partners for support and business calls. The VoIP application, an Asterisk PBX, includes the phone menus and underlying logic used when calling ViaTalk or HostRocket support, a callback service, and employee voicemail. The web application provides an overview of current callers and employes, as well as a detailed history of incoming and outgoing calls.

On average, ViaTalk PBX handles 500-1000 calls daily and has nearly 100% uptime since deployment in August 2008.

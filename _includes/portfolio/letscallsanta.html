Let's Call Santa

Ruby, Rails, Javascript, jQuery, MySQL, PHP, CodeIgniter

I created the Rails app that powers Let's Call Santa. Let's Call Santa is a fun service offered during the holidays that allows children to call Santa at the North Pole and leave him a voicemail with their wish list.

I originally created Let's Call Santa in 2009 using PHP and CodeIgniter. When ViaTalk revamped the service in 2010, I rewrote it in Ruby On Rails. Let's Call Santa is one of my favorite projects to date.
